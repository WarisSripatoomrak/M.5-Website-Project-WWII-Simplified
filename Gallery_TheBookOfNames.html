The Book of Names (At Auschwitz-Birkenau)

          The Book of Names makes up the permanent exhibit at Auschwitz-Birkenau honouring the Jewish victims. It pushes visitors beyond their knowledge of the facts of the Final Solution to the Jewish Question. The book contains every victim's name known, which has more than 4,000,000 names.
